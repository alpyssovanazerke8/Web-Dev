Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 3
Grated Parmesan Cheese 50g
Garlic 1 clove
Salt & Pepper To taste

Steps:

1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.

3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then mix in the Parmesan cheese.

4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some cooking water. Add the spaghetti to the pan with pancetta and mix.

5. Remove from heat and stir in the egg mixture, adding reserved water if needed to create a creamy sauce.

6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.

Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Milk 300ml
Eggs 2
Butter 50g
Sugar 1 tbsp
Salt Pinch

Steps:

1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.

2. Beat the eggs, then add milk and mix well.

3. Gradually add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients, stirring to form a smooth batter.

4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and melt a little butter.

5. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook the other side.

6. Repeat until all the batter is used. Serve with your favorite toppings.
