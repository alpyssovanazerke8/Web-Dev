  • They're intelligent!
  • Great navigators!

Top 3 most famous pigeons

  1. Cher Ami (hero of World War I)
  2. The homing pigeon
  3. The passenger pigeon (though extinct, its story is famous)

Basic info

Pigeons are birds with smooth feathers and a gentle cooing sound. They are often found in cities, living alongside humans. Pigeons have incredible homing abilities and have been used to carry messages for centuries.
